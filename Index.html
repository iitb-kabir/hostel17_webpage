General Announcements

New Facility: 6th Floor Study Room Open

We're pleased to announce that the 6th Floor Study Room is now open! It is equipped with new furniture and electrical connections for a quiet study environment. Access is exclusively for residents via the biometric system.

Posted October 18, 2025

Mandatory: Cycle Stickers & Biometric Installation

All residents must collect cycle stickers (compulsory for cycle owners) and complete biometric installation (mandatory for common room access). Please follow the schedule provided.

Posted August 24, 2025

PHO Cycle Segregation Drive & Strict Deadline

The Public Health Office (PHO) is conducting a scrap cycle drive. Claim your cycle by putting a sticker on it before September 23, 2025. Unclaimed cycles will be removed by PHO without extension.

Posted September 23, 2025

Room Allocation Complete & Shifting Guidelines

Room allocation is complete. Check with the Hall Manager for the list. Shifting is allowed, but remember to bring one passport-size photo and fill out the room vacate form from your previous hostel beforehand.

Posted July 16, 2025

Hostel Facilities

Study Rooms: Two dedicated quiet spaces: 6th Floor (newly opened) and 3rd Floor.

TV Room: Common room for entertainment and screenings.

Laundry & Ironing: Paid service and a separate ironing room.

Lobby Area: Ideal for guests or casual seating near the entrance.

Reading Material: Standies provided with daily newspapers and magazines.

Emergency Equipment

Fire extinguishers are located near the lifts on all floors. Fire alarms are clearly marked throughout the building.
