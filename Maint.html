Security & Access Guidelines

General Security Rules

  • Identification: Always carry your hostel ID and be prepared to present it when requested by staff.
  • Locks: Always lock your room when leaving to ensure the safety and security of your personal belongings.
  • Curfew: Residents must adhere to the established curfews or entry/exit times set by the hostel administration.
